Wolverine - Logan Howlett

Origins & Backstory

Born as James Howlett in the 19th century, Wolverine's past is shrouded in tragedy. After discovering his mutant abilities as a child, he adopted the name Logan and lived through decades of war, loss, and betrayal.

Abilities & Powers

Wolverine possesses an accelerated healing factor, which makes him nearly immortal. His retractable adamantium claws and enhanced senses make him a formidable warrior, capable of taking down even the most powerful enemies.
