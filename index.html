Wolverine - Logan Howlett

Logan Howlett, also known as Wolverine, is one of the most iconic mutants in the X-Men universe. With his adamantium claws, animal-like senses, and remarkable healing factor, Wolverine has become a symbol of resilience and strength.

First appearing in "The Incredible Hulk #180" in 1974, Wolverine quickly became a fan favorite. Hugh Jackman's portrayal of the character in the X-Men film series has further cemented Wolverine's place in pop culture.

Whether he's fighting alongside the X-Men or embarking on solo adventures, Wolverine's journey is one of pain, loss, and redemption. His rugged personality, unbreakable spirit, and loyalty to his friends make him a truly unforgettable anti-hero.
